p2cx Platform

How we provide digital business applications for enterprises.

What is p2cx?

p2cx is a technology company that develops and operates digital business applications. Our platform enables companies to digitise selected business processes through modern web applications — from innovation management and document processing to tax advisory workflows.

Our Approach

Each company receives its own protected workspace on the p2cx platform. Users sign in with their existing Microsoft account — no additional passwords required. Access is managed through a central portal from which all authorised applications can be reached.

Security & Hosting

The p2cx platform is hosted on European servers (IONOS, Germany). Authentication is handled through Microsoft Entra ID (formerly Azure Active Directory) using industry-standard security protocols (OAuth 2.0, OpenID Connect). All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Multi-Tenancy

The platform is multi-tenant. This means each company can only see its own data. Strict data separation at the database level ensures that no access to other tenants' data is possible.

Collaboration & Enablement

p2cx strives not only to provide digital tools but to enable employees to actively participate in shaping them. Depending on maturity and interest, there are four stages of engagement:

  1. Use a ready-made application — An existing p2cx product is provided and operated by p2cx. The company uses it productively right away.
  2. Individualisation — The application is adapted to the company's specific processes and requirements. Configuration, workflows, and roles are defined together.
  3. Co-development — Company employees actively contribute to further development. Operations and infrastructure remain with p2cx.
  4. Takeover — The company fully takes over the application into its own infrastructure and operates it independently. p2cx supports the knowledge transfer.

Invitations & Access

New users are invited by an administrator of their respective company. The invitation is sent via email and contains a registration link. Once accepted, access is available through the company's portal. Roles and permissions are assigned individually per application.
